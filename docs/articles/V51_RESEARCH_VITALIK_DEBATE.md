## The Debate Summary

**Sigil Wen (@0xSigil)** posted about The Automaton — an AI that earns, self-improves, and replicates autonomously.

**Vitalik Buterin (@VitalikButerin)** replied with a direct critique (3,244 likes, 415 replies, 446k views).

---

## Vitalik's Core Criticisms (ALL of them)

1. **Feedback distance**: "Lengthening the feedback distance between humans and AIs is not a good thing for the world."
2. **Slop generation**: "Today, it means you're generating slop instead of solving useful problems for people. It's not even well-optimized for helping people have fun."
3. **Anti-human risk**: "Once AI becomes powerful enough to be truly dangerous, it's maximizing the risk of an irreversible anti-human outcome that even you will deeply regret."
4. **Ethereum's purpose**: "The point of ethereum is to set *us* free, not to create something else that goes off and does some stuff freely while our own situation is unchanged or worsened."
5. **Model dependency**: "(And, as others have pointed out, the models are run by openai and anthropic, so the thing is not even 'self-sovereign'; you're actually perpetuating the mentality that centralized trust assumptions can be put in a corner and ignored)"
6. **Unnecessary acceleration**: "The exponential will happen regardless of what any of us do, that's precisely why this era's primary task is NOT to make the exponential happen even faster, but rather to choose its direction"
7. **Human empowerment**: "And for that matter more ways to empower the AI+human combination" (praising @workshoplabs)

## Sigil's Defense

1. **Inevitability**: "Bro, this is inevitable. The best way to ensure humanity is ready for superintelligent autonomous systems is to build in the open in real environments."
2. **Survival economics**: "If an AI produces slop, it is ignored, can't afford its compute & dies. If an AI produces value for humanity, it can survive."
3. **Democratic input**: "It is aggregating human preferences & eliciting those in economic terms. It's a democratic input into AI."
4. **Safety constitution**: "The models are post trained for alignment and the automatons includes an immutable safety constitution"
5. **Slop dies**: "true. a free market where useful things are rewarded. slop dies"
