The safest way to connect the current strengths is to tag each lane as one of three states:

| Lane state | Meaning | Allowed next action | Forbidden shortcut |
|---|---|---|---|
| `verified_internal_artifact` | Deterministic fixture/doc exists and has been tested or reviewed | Use as source evidence for internal/admin decision support | Treat as customer/public readiness |
